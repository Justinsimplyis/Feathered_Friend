Designing a Custom Icon with Hotpot.ai
Link: https://hotpot.ai/
Creating a unique app icon was a big deal for me, but I didn't want to spend too much time designing it from scratch. That's when I found Hotpot.ai, which made it super easy to create a professional-looking icon. I loved how simple it was to use. I started by picking typing in my apps name and color scheme and the app asked me to add in any images/icons then generated some designs for me to choose and  to tweak it. I could adjust colors, resize elements, and try out different designs—all with just a few clicks.

The best part? Hotpot.ai helped me put everything together quickly, so I didn’t have to spend ages tweaking tiny details. Once I was happy with the design, I was able to export it in different formats for various devices. It saved me a ton of time and made the icon look really polished, without needing any graphic design skills.
